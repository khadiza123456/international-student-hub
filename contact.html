Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond within 24 hours on business days. For urgent inquiries, please call our support line.

Do you charge for consultation services?

Initial consultations are completely free. We only charge for specialized services like document preparation and application processing.

Can you help with scholarship applications?

Yes! We provide comprehensive scholarship search assistance and application guidance to help you secure funding.

What countries do you specialize in?

We specialize in USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, Japan, and many other popular study destinations.

Do you offer visa assistance?

Yes, we provide complete visa guidance including document checklist, application review, and interview preparation.

Can I schedule an in-person meeting?

Absolutely! You can visit any of our global offices or schedule a virtual meeting via Zoom/Google Meet.
